This short course will equip scientists with the tools and approaches required to use ML in their own reserach. This course will include both applied and theoretical topics in ML/LLM research, delivered across multiple lectures, seminars and computational practicals. In order to ensure that students are properly prepared for the course, then should complete our "Pre-Course Guide" first, by going through each of the sections shown on the left of the screen, starting with 'Checklist'.
